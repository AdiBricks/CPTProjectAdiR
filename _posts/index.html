Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why should I create an account?

Because it's more fun when the computer knows your gaming secrets! Plus, who doesn't love personalized game recommendations from a machine?

Follow the floating bubbles, they are the wise sages of game recommendations. Or you could just check our Games section, that works too!

What happens if I press the star button?

A star is born! Just kidding. It changes the background color and throws a mini firework celebration because we believe every click is a cause for celebration.

Is the snowfall button a time machine?

Unfortunately, no. It just makes it snow on our website. But imagine how cool it would be if it were a time machine!

So how do you use this site?

Go to our games list and play some free games! Make sure to create a free account to play even cooler ones!
